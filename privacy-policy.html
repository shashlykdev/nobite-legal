Privacy Policy for Nobite

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect any personal data.
  • Apple manages in-app purchases and subscriptions and may collect data according to their privacy policy.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identifying information
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings or sound data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Device identifiers

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • App preferences and settings (frequency, volume, sound mode)
  • Custom schedules and timers
  • Usage statistics and protection history
  • Daily usage tracking for free tier limits
  • Theme preferences
  • Premium subscription status (managed via Apple)

Keychain Storage

We use iOS Keychain to securely store daily usage data. This data:

  • Persists across app reinstalls to prevent abuse of the free tier
  • Is stored securely on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to external servers

Data Transmission

  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Subscription information is managed through Apple's App Store
  • No personal data leaves your device

Your Rights

  • All local data (except Keychain) is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can reset your data anytime through the app's settings
  • No opt-out required as we don't collect analytics or tracking data

Contact

For privacy questions, email: hello@shashlyk.dev

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
