Subscription Terms for Nobite Premium

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Subscription Products

  • Annual Plan: $4.99/year - Auto-renewable subscription
  • Lifetime: $7.99 - One-time purchase (Best Value)

Premium Features

  • Unlimited Daily Usage: No 10-minute daily limit
  • Custom Themes: Access to all 8 app themes (Ocean, Aurora, Sky, Dreamscape, Sunset, Warmth, Vivid)
  • Smart Scheduling: Set automatic protection schedules for different times and days
  • Background Protection: Keep protection running while using other apps

Free Tier

  • 10 minutes of daily protection (resets at midnight)
  • All 5 sound modes (Standard, Sweep, Pulsed, Multi-Frequency, Adaptive)
  • Full frequency range (15-22 kHz)
  • Volume control
  • Default theme only

Auto-Renewal Terms (Annual Plan)

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends
  • Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • The subscription will renew at the same price unless price changes are communicated in advance

Managing Subscriptions

  • Manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings
  • Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iOS device
  • Subscriptions can also be managed at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

Cancellation Policy

  • No refunds for unused portions of subscription periods
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • Premium features remain accessible until subscription expires
  • Lifetime purchases do not expire

Free Trial (if applicable)

  • Free trial automatically converts to paid subscription unless cancelled
  • Cancel during trial period to avoid charges

Price Changes

We may change subscription prices with 30 days notice. Price changes don't affect existing subscribers until their next renewal.

Refunds

Refund requests must be made through Apple's App Store refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com.
