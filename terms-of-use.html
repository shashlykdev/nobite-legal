App Description

Nobite is a sound-based mosquito deterrent app that emits ultrasonic frequencies (15-22 kHz) intended to repel mosquitoes. The app offers multiple sound modes and customizable settings.

App Features

  • 5 Sound Modes: Standard, Frequency Sweep, Pulsed, Multi-Frequency, and Adaptive
  • Adjustable Frequency: 15 kHz to 22 kHz range
  • Volume Control: Adjustable output level
  • 8 Themes: Customizable app appearance (Premium feature)
  • Smart Scheduling: Automated protection schedules (Premium feature)
  • Background Protection: Continue protection while using other apps (Premium feature)
