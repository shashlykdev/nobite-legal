Getting Started

How to Use Nobite

  1. Start Protection: Tap the large shield button on the main screen to activate mosquito protection.
  2. Adjust Frequency: Use the slider to adjust the ultrasonic frequency between 15-22 kHz.
  3. Choose Sound Mode: Select from 5 different sound patterns in Settings.
  4. Ensure Volume: Make sure your device volume is turned up for optimal effectiveness.
Pro Tip
The app works best with device speakers. For outdoor use, place your device in a central location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this app really repel mosquitoes?
Nobite generates ultrasonic frequencies that some studies suggest may deter mosquitoes. However, effectiveness can vary based on mosquito species, environment, and device capabilities. We recommend using the app as a supplementary measure alongside traditional repellent methods.
Is it safe to use around pets?
While the frequencies used are generally considered safe, some pets (especially dogs and cats) may be sensitive to ultrasonic sounds. Monitor your pets' behavior when first using the app and discontinue use if they show signs of distress.
Why can't I hear anything when the app is running?
The app produces ultrasonic frequencies (15-22 kHz) which are at or above the range of human hearing. Most adults cannot hear these frequencies, but the sounds are designed to affect mosquitoes. This is normal behavior.
What's the difference between free and premium?
Free: 10 minutes daily protection, all 5 sound modes, full frequency range, default theme.

Premium: Unlimited daily usage, 8 custom themes, smart scheduling, background protection.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any previous premium purchases associated with your Apple ID.
Why does my daily limit persist after reinstalling?
To ensure fair usage, we store daily usage data securely in the iOS Keychain, which persists across app reinstalls. This resets automatically at midnight local time each day.
Can I use other apps while Nobite is running?
Yes, with Premium you can use background protection. However, other audio apps may interfere with the ultrasonic playback. For best results, avoid using other audio apps simultaneously.
Which sound mode should I use?
  • Standard: Constant frequency - good for general use
  • Sweep: Varies frequency - may be more effective in some environments
  • Pulsed: Intermittent bursts - saves battery
  • Multi-Frequency: Multiple frequencies - broader coverage
  • Adaptive: Random variations - prevents mosquito adaptation

Troubleshooting

No Sound / Protection Not Working

  • Check that your device volume is turned up
  • Ensure the app isn't muted
  • Close other audio apps that might interfere
  • Restart the app

App Crashes

  • Ensure you have the latest version installed
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app if issues persist
  • Contact support with details about the crash

Purchase Issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your App Store account is signed in
  • Try "Restore Purchases" in Settings
  • Request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com

Scheduling Not Working (Premium)

  • Ensure notifications are enabled for the app
  • Check that Background App Refresh is enabled
  • Disable Low Power Mode
